About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate Design and Visual Communication (DKV) student with a keen eye for detail and creative problem-solving skills. I believe in the power of visual storytelling and its ability to connect people, ideas, and emotions.

My journey in design began when I discovered my passion for transforming concepts into visually compelling stories. Since then, I've been honing my skills in various design disciplines, from graphic design to illustration and digital media.

I approach each project with curiosity and enthusiasm, always seeking to understand the core message before translating it into visual language. My design philosophy centers around simplicity, functionality, and emotional impact.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring art exhibitions, photographing urban landscapes, or experimenting with new creative techniques that I can incorporate into my professional work.
